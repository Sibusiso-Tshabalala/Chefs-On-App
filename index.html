Our App

Book a private chef, plan your event, or order meal prep all in one app.

  1. Select Menu & Book
    Browse a variety of menus crafted by professional chefs. Choose your preferred dishes, date, and time — then confirm your booking in just a few taps.
  2. Your Chef Shops & Cooks
    Once booked, your chef takes care of everything — from sourcing high-quality ingredients to arriving fully prepared for your event.
  3. Enjoy Your Meal
    Sit back, relax, and indulge in restaurant-quality dining experience — right at your home or event venue.
  4. Chef Cleans Up
    When the meal is over, your chef handles the cleanup — leaving your space spotless and you free to enjoy the moment.
